Coastal Erosion Model

Formation of Cracks, Caves, Arches, Stacks, and Stumps

Stage 1 of 5

The Weakness

Erosion starts at a crack or joint in a headland of resistant rock (like limestone or chalk). This weakness is exploited by marine processes.

Hydraulic Action

Air trapped in cracks by waves explodes outwards, breaking rock.

Abrasion

Waves fling sand and pebbles against the cliff base like sandpaper.

Freeze-Thaw

Water freezes in cracks, expands, and shatters rock from within.
